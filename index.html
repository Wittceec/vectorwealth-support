Frequently Asked Questions

Is this real money?

No. Vector Wealth is a paper trading simulator. All trades, balances, and positions are virtual. It is designed for strategy practice and education, not actual brokerage execution.

Where does the market data come from?

We use real-time and end-of-day data from professional market providers (Alpaca and Alpha Vantage). Prices reflect actual market movements for US stocks and ETFs.

How do Sentry Rules work?

Sentry rules are automated checks that run once per day. If a stock meets your criteria (e.g., "Down more than 3%"), the app sends you a notification so you can review your strategy.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Vector Wealth uses anonymous authentication. We do not require your email, name, or bank links. Your strategy is your business.
