Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

Vector Wealth ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Vector Wealth mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Vector Wealth is designed to be as private as possible. We do not require an account, email address, or phone number to use the app.

  • Anonymous IDs: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to generate a unique, non-identifiable ID for your device. This allows your portfolio and rules to sync to the cloud without us knowing who you are.
  • Portfolio Data: We store your virtual positions, trades, and sentry rules in a secure database to provide app functionality.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous crash reports and performance data to improve the application.

2. No Financial Integration

Vector Wealth DOES NOT connect to your real bank accounts or brokerages. We do not collect financial account numbers, passwords, or transaction history from real-world financial institutions.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide the app:

  • Google Firebase: For data storage and anonymous authentication.
  • Alpaca/Alpha Vantage: For real-time market data.
  • Sentry: For crash reporting and bug tracking.

4. Data Retention

Your data remains on our servers as long as the app is installed on your device or until you choose to delete your account from the settings menu. Once deleted, all associated portfolio data is permanently wiped from our systems.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@vectorwealth.app.
